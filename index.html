📁 Folder Sync

Sync your todos to a local folder. Use a cloud-synced folder (Google Drive, OneDrive, Dropbox) to access your todos across devices.

Setup:
1. Install Google Drive, OneDrive, or Dropbox desktop app
2. Click "Choose Folder" below and pick a folder inside your synced drive
3. Your todos will auto-save to that folder as a JSON file
4. On another device, choose the same synced folder to load your data
